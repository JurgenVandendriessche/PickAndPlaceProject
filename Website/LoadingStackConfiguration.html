Loading a stack configuration

Imagine you have two PCB's that need the same components. You have spend some time changing all reels in the program before exporting the first PCB and now you need that same setup again (or part of the setup).You load your files all footprints are known of course everything goes great and then you click on the generate button but the reels aren't in the right order. Good news, you can load your previous setup again. in 2 simple steps.

  1. Click on the Load stack configuration button
  2. Go to the exported file from your previous PCB and open it.
  3. See how the reels change to the correct position and enjoy.

NOTE: Locked reels are not moved when you load a stack configuration.
